About the Demo

This is a demo hackathon registration page for Rokini Institute. Participants will propose new educational solutions with a focus on adaptive learning, content delivery, mentorship, and learner motivation. Your team can choose any of the problem statements below and register to showcase a concept, prototype, or user experience design.

Problem Statements

1. Personalized Learning Path Engine

Create a recommendation engine that suggests the next course or learning path for a student based on their goals, existing skills, and preferences.

2. Adaptive Progress Dashboard

Design a dashboard that visualizes learner progress, highlights strengths and weaknesses, and adapts the curriculum to improve outcomes.

3. AI Mentor Chatbot

Build a conversational mentor assistant that answers questions, provides study prompts, and guides learners through course material.

4. Gamified Certification Path

Develop a gamification model for certificate journeys, where students earn badges, milestones, and rewards as they complete learning milestones.

5. Microlearning Scheduler

Invent a microlearning planner that schedules short, daily learning activities for students and helps them stay consistent.
