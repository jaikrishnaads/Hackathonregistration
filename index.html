About the Demo

This is a demo hackathon registration page for Rohini Institute. Participants will propose new educational solutions with a focus on adaptive learning, content delivery, mentorship, and learner motivation. Your team can choose any of the problem statements below and register to showcase a concept, prototype, or user experience design.

Problem Statements

1. Learning Management System (LMS)

Design a comprehensive learning management system (LMS) that optimizes core digital classroom operations and tracks student engagement through the following requirements:

📁 Upload internship materials
🎥 Conduct online classes
📝 Share assignments
👤 Track student attendance
📊 Conduct quizzes and assessments
🎓 Issue certificates

2. Adaptive Progress Dashboard

Design a dashboard that visualizes learner progress, highlights strengths and weaknesses, and adapts the curriculum to improve outcomes.

3. Microlearning Scheduler

Invent a microlearning planner that schedules short, daily learning activities for students and helps them stay consistent.

1. AI Mentor Chatbot

Build a conversational mentor assistant that answers questions, provides study prompts, and guides learners through course material.

2. Personalized Learning Path Engine

Create an AI-driven recommendation engine that suggests the next course or learning path for a student based on their goals, existing skills, and preferences.
